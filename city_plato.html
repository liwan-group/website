City-PLATO

Platform for Land‑use Analysis and Transparent Outcomes

City-PLATO is a AI-enabled planning intelligence platform that brings together developers, local planning authorities, and residents — making land-use planning more transparent, efficient, and participatory.

Why we exist

Our Mission

We believe that great cities are built through collaboration. City-PLATO exists to bridge the gap between planning professionals, decision-makers, and the communities they serve. By combining cutting-edge AI analysis with open community feedback, we make planning processes legible and accessible to everyone — so that land-use decisions reflect the needs of all stakeholders, not just a few.

Platform

What We Build

One platform, three powerful perspectives.

🏗️

For Developers

Streamline pre-application preparation with AI-assisted document analysis. Identify promising development opportunities and gain land-valuation insights — faster and with greater confidence.

🏛️

For Planning Authorities

Augment limited internal capacity with AI-assisted early-stage planning discussions and spatial analysis. Identify optimal residential, commercial, and mixed-use zones to guide sustainable growth.

🏘️

For Communities

Discover what is being planned in your neighbourhood, share your views, and make your voice count. Optionally allow your feedback to inform future planning decisions — planning democracy made real.
